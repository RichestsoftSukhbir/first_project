About Us

'NZUKO' means ‘coming together’ in Igbo. Our mission is to celebrate the global Igbo experience and make our rich heritage more accessible to the world through storytelling and research.

We're building an inclusive community where folks can authentically connect and engage with the past, present, and future of the Igbo experience through our content and products.
